This game is all about attacking the bees and making their health zero. You can attack the bees by clicking on the Hit button. The bees have health points from strongest to weakest. Each bee type will take different damage when hit:

  • Queen Bee: 8 damage
  • Worker Bee: 10 damage
  • Drone Bee: 12 damage

Once a group of bees is destroyed they will start attacking back when you miss a hit on them. Each hit they do will reduce your health by 1 point.

The game is finished on two conditions:

  • When all the bees are destroyed (or when the queen is destroyed)
  • When your health is zero

You can leave the game whenever you like and come back to continue playing from where you left off.

Also, you can add your name if you click on "Player Name" under the "Hit" button.
